This is an unofficial, fan-made edition of William Cullen Bryant’s 1871 translation of the Odyssey, prepared as a reading companion to the audiobook narrated in the artificial voice of Sir Michael Caine (ElevenLabs, 2026). Throughout the text, the names of the gods and heroes have been restored to their Greek forms so that the printed page matches the names spoken in that narration. It is a derivative work built upon the Standard Ebooks edition of Bryant’s translation; it is not an official Standard Ebooks release and carries no endorsement from Standard Ebooks, which is not responsible for the name changes or any other alteration made in this edition.

That edition is the product of many hours of hard work by volunteers for Standard Ebooks, and builds on the hard work of other literature lovers made possible by the public domain.

It is based on digital scans from various sources.
